Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

cln. by Risk Creatives respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

cln. does not collect any personal information, photos, or location data. Your photos stay on your device. We do not upload or store them externally.

2. Analytics

We may use anonymous usage statistics (such as how often the app is opened) to improve cln. This data is non-identifiable and used solely for internal insights.

3. Photo Library Access

cln. requires permission to access your photo library in order to allow swiping and cleaning functionality. This access remains on-device only.

4. No Ads. No Trackers.

We do not use ad networks or third-party trackers. cln. is built to respect your time, space, and privacy.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Material changes will be communicated in-app.

6. Contact

Questions? Reach out at support@riskcreatives.com.

Thank you for trusting cln.
